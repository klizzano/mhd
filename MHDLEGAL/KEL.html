Kristen E. Lizzano

Kristen E. Lizzano is admitted to the bars of Pennsylvania and New Jersey. Prior to joining March, Hurwitz and DeMarco, P.C., Ms. Lizzano worked extensively in the fields of bankruptcy and collection. She obtained a B.A. in History, with a minor in Legal Studies from the University of Delaware. Following college, Ms. Lizzano worked in the Clerk’s Office of the United States Bankruptcy Court for the Eastern District of Pennsylvania. While continuing to work, she attended law school at Widener University – Delaware Law School. Upon completion of law school, Ms. Lizzano practiced primarily in the area of collections, with a heavy focus on attaining results through the use of alternative as well as traditional legal methods.

Since joining March, Hurwitz & DeMarco, P.C. in 2011, Ms. Lizzano has focused her practice on civil litigation, insurance defense, collections, wills and estate planning. She enjoys the challenge of employing both traditional and creative means for obtaining the goals of her clients.

Originally from New Jersey, Ms. Lizzano currently resides in Newtown Square, Pennsylvania with her husband. Outside of work, Ms. Lizzano is active is the local community theater scene, both on stage and off, and has been a costume designer/lead seamstress for many productions. She is also very involved in Marple Presbyterian Church where she is a Youth Group Leader and a member of the Church’s choir.
