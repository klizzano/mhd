Gary A. Hurwitz

Gary A. Hurwitz is a graduate of Franklin & Marshall College and Villanova University School of Law. Mr. Hurwitz joined the firm in 1976 upon his admission to the bars of Pennsylvania and New Jersey.

Mr. Hurwitz has a broad range of trial experience including nearly every form of civil litigation and criminal Federal RICO Cases. Mr. Hurwitz has handled complex commercial litigation, environmental mass tort litigation, municipal law, multi-district litigation, professional liability, product liability, employment and federal civil rights litigation on behalf of companies and school districts as well as wrongful death cases. He is a member of the Million and Multi-million Dollar Advocates Forum.

Mr. Hurwitz is the father of two daughters and is married to Holly Ruckdeschel, Ph.D.
