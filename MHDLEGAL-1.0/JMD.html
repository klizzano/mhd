Joseph M. DeMarco

Joseph M. DeMarco was born in Philadelphia and has been a long-time resident of Delaware County. He attended Swarthmore College and Widener University - Delaware Law School where he graduated cum laude and was a law review member. Mr. DeMarco clerked for the firm during law school and continued with the firm upon admission to the Pennsylvania Bar in 1985.

Mr. DeMarco has spent the last 30+ years as a civil trial lawyer where he has successfully tried cases to verdict from across the entire spectrum and in venues throughout the eastern and central parts of the state.
